Our goals

We want to embrace the relationship that humans and try to make the relationship even stronger! Our motto... Pets and their owners unite to bring the world far more delight. Right now, many pets struggle to find a home that suites them. It is also PetsPortal goal to stop this, to get as many pets as possible a safe place to live.

How will we accomplish our goals?

The best method to have pets and owners bond with each other is to make sure owners even have pets in the first place! We try to get as many pets adopted to owners that suit them. To do this, we take most of our profits and send them to pet shelters and other associations that can use the money to benefit pets.

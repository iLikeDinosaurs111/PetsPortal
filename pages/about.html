About Us

Woof! Welcome to PetsPortal! I'm Teddy, our mascot. PetsPortal is a place where pets and their owners can meet and bond with each other. Our goals are to strengthen the relationship between people and pets through fun and amusement! Woof woof!

Hi, I'm Arjun! I am the original creator of PetsPortal. I have been wanting a pet for so long, and over time, I realized how lucky people with pets are! They have their own friend that is ready to take care of them at any time and loves them in ways others can't! I wanted to take this relationshi between pets and their owners and strengthen it even more to maximize the appreciation and enjoyments of pets.

Meow! I'm Teddy's friend, Theo. Teddy told me a bit about how PetsPortal started. It was actually for a challenge from FBLA! PetsPortal is an entry for a website devlopment event hosted by the Future Buisness Leaders of America. FBLA is an association for the purpose of helping children prepare to become financial leaders to help the community and world! To learn more, try contacting us personally!
